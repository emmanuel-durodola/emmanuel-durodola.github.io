About Me

I am a graduate student in the Physics and Astronomy department at Dartmouth College currently using observations from the James Webb Space Telecope to better understand black hole - galaxy coevolution in the early universe.

Research

I am broadly interested in black hole evolution. More specifically, I am interested in accretion phenomena, Active Galctic Nuclei, black hole seeding and black hole - host galaxy coevolution.
